Developing **mobile intelligence software for autonomous navigation of legged robots** in dynamic and atypical environments for real-world deployment, funded by **KEIT**.

My focus is on implementing **multi-robot autonomous navigation and path planning** for agricultural field robots operating in actual farm environments, where terrain, lighting, and obstacle distributions differ significantly from controlled indoor settings.
