We participated in the **Quadruped Robot Challenges (QRC)** held at ICRA 2025, organized by the IEEE Robotics and Automation Society, and earned **2nd place**.

The competition required a legged robot to autonomously navigate complex, multi-stage environments while performing perception, planning, and manipulation tasks under strict time constraints.
